How it's calculated

Pre-season uses career history only. History fades out over weeks 1–3 (40% / 25% / 10%), then it's fully in-season from week 4. In weeks 1–3 the in-season factor set is reduced: Form joins at week 4 (with under 3 games it's identical to Record), and Conference joins at week 3 for division leagues.
